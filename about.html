About ShopVerse

Who We Are

ShopVerse is a front-end e-commerce platform where you can browse categories such as shoes, clothes, electronics, books, toys, and furniture. Shop deals, add items to your cart, and checkout with a Flipkart-style billing experience.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver a clean, modern, and user-friendly online shopping experience inspired by leading platforms like Amazon and Flipkart — built for learning web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
